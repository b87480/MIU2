WEAPONS

BAZOOKA

THE BAZOOKA:

Great for killing multiple zombies at one time!

The bazooka has a very high kill ratio but very slow re-load times!

CROWBAR

THE CROWBAR:

Easy to use, no reloading, and fairly light weight.

The crowbar can also be used for many different things as well, like opening cans of beans or beer bottles!

The crowbar should only be used in a worse case scenario! It takes a lot of hits to the head with this weapon to kill a zombie!! This is not Hollywood people!

GRENADE

THE GRENADE:

The grenade is awesome!!!!, but you only get to use it once!

And once again this is not Hollywood. Grenades do not blow up city blocks. It is a very small explosion that will kill zombies within a 5 to 10 foot radius.

Unless your awesome and throw it at a gas station! The grenade should be a back up weapon at the very most. Save it till the end and maybe you can use it as a really cool firework show when all the zombies are dead!!!!

GUN

THE GUN:

Great choice of weapon. But it depends on your aim, and how much ammo you have for it.

The gun is easy to carry, use, clean and reload. Head shots with this weapon is ideal so practice, practice, practice!

DO NOT TRY AND OPEN BEERS WITH THE GUN!

This does not work well and you will most likely make a lot of your commrades angry!

KNIFE

THE KNIFE:

The must have for any zombie killer!

But, be fore warned that killing zombies with a knife is messy and takes a lot of power and stamina. So if you are planning on using a knife you better practice with it and exercise like a beast!

The knife is also a silent killer, so it won't attract a lot of attention from other zombies.

This weapon is also great for opening things, cutting things and yes you guessed it OPENS BEERS!

    JOSHUA WHITNEY

    Born: California
    Live: Asheville NC
    Loves: Family, Crossfit, Programming, Web Design, Mountain Biking.

    A Little Bit About Myself:

    I was born on the west coast and raised on the east coast. I've always had a strong passion for technology. My dad raised me around computers, he was a programmer as well and started me out on a Coomodore 64!

    Throughout my life I have done a lot of things and I never thought programming would have been one of them. Although I am still new to the actual programming side of technology I have found a passion in it like no other. I hope to continue to learn and improve and build a successful career.

    ABOUT ZOMBIE HIT LIST:

    Basically I thought it would be fun to build an app where people could register to become a zombie killer and add hated zombies to a hit list. The app is all in good fun. With all the hype around zombies these days, why not? So if you see a zombie make sure to register and add them to the Zombie Hit List!
